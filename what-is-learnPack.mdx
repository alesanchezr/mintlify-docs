## 🚀 A Journey of Transformation

LearnPack started as a **coding sidekick**, interactive, auto-graded exercises inside developer environments.

By 2025, it has evolved into an **[AI-powered course builder](https://www.learnpack.co/)** for any subject, accessible in the browser. Whether it is coding, marketing, psychology, or history, LearnPack will turn raw material into a **structured learning experience**. **No technical background required.**

![LearnPack overview](../images/learnpack-home.png)

<Callout type="info">
<span style={{color:"#4F46E5"}}><b>Why this matters:</b></span> instead of collecting scattered tutorials, LearnPack creates a clear, measurable learning path and pairs it with contextual tutoring.
</Callout>

LearnPack began in programming education and grew into a **general-purpose authoring platform**. Upload a **PDF**, **slides**, or a **video transcript** (or start from a blank idea) and generate a **production-ready course** with lessons, quizzes, projects, and interactive tasks.

## 🔍 Cutting Through the Noise

The internet is overflowing with information, choosing a path can be paralyzing. LearnPack **creates structure**:

- **University PDF:** get a complete course: lessons, quizzes, projects.
- **No material:** say “digital marketing from zero,” “history of philosophy,” or “Python basics,” and it will **generate an interactive course from scratch**.
- **Progress tracking:** your progress is **measured as you go**, so learning is trackable.

## ✅ How LearnPack works (at a glance)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Describe or upload">
    Start from a goal (what you want to learn or teach), or attach <b>PDFs</b>, <b>slides</b>, or <b>videos</b> with transcript.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate the course">
    LearnPack drafts <b>lessons</b>, <b>quizzes</b>, and <b>projects</b> tailored to your objective.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refine with AI">
    Ask to <b>rewrite</b>, <b>simplify</b>, <b>translate</b>, or <b>add activities</b> anywhere.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish anywhere">
    Share a URL, export <b>SCORM</b> for your LMS, or <b>embed via iFrame</b>.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## 🤖 A Mentor Built In

Courses are only half the story. Every learner has doubts, gets stuck, or needs feedback. That is why LearnPack comes with **Rigobot**, our AI mentor that transforms a learning experience into a **hyper-personalized learning experience**.

![Rigobot mentor illustration](../images/rigo.png)

[Rigobot](https://4geeksacademy.com/us/ai-powered-learning/rigobot-ai-coding-mentor) sees the exact lesson, project, or exercise you are working on. It uses **context-aware hints** and **evaluation algorithms**, never giving away the solution, but nudging you in the right direction. It **remembers your history**, **adapts to your pace**, and **measures your growth**.

## Real Examples in Action

- **Marketing Basics in 2 Hours:** quick lessons on branding and ads with auto-graded quizzes.  
- **History of World Wars:** interactive timelines and knowledge checks to grasp complex events.

## 📝 Why This Matters

Beginners, career changers, and teachers no longer need to curate resources or invent structure. **LearnPack handles the foundation** so the focus stays on learning.

- **Anywhere access:** fully browser-based.  
- **Personalized support:** Rigobot guides you along the way.  
- **Open-source adaptability:** freedom to shape courses as needed.

Impact so far: **4,000+ learners across six countries** and a **79% hiring rate within 140 days** (mid-2025).

💡 <b>Tip:</b> ship a small course first, then iterate with AI: “add a quiz”, “simplify this section”, “translate to Spanish”, or “add recap images”.
</Callout>
