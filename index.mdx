## Build a course in four steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Describe or upload">
    Start from a goal <span style={{color:"#4F46E5"}}>(what to teach/learn)</span> or attach <b>PDFs</b>, <b>slides</b>, or <b>videos</b> (with transcript).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate the course">
    LearnPack drafts lessons, quizzes, and projects tailored to your objective.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refine with AI">
    Ask to <b>simplify</b>, <b>translate</b>, or <b>add a quiz/diagram/image</b> anywhere.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish anywhere">
    Share a URL, export <b>SCORM</b>, or <b>embed via iFrame</b>.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## LearnPack's AI Tutor (Rigobot) ðŸ¤–

<Callout type="info">
Rigobot is the tutor inside every course. It will deliver a **hyper-personalized learning experience** with **memory**, **context awareness**, and **guided reasoning**.
</Callout>

| Mode | What it does | Use it for | Read |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Core model** | **Context:** sees the current step, **memory:** recalls attempts and progress, **on-screen help:** guidance in the same view. | A clear picture of how tutoring will appear across lessons, quizzes, and projects. | [How Rigobot works](/rigobot/how-rigobot-works) |
| **Contextual hints, Socratic** | **Hints over answers:** targeted questions, **reasoning:** builds understanding, **focus:** reduces solution peeking. | A learner is stuck on a step or concept and needs a nudge, not the solution. | [Contextual guidance and Socratic learning](/rigobot/contextual-guidance-and-socratic-learning) |
| **Multilingual, accessible** | **Language switching:** preserves context, **accessible text:** clear, stepwise interactions. | Mixed language cohorts, accessibility requirements, simplified wording. | [Multilingual tutoring and accessibility](/rigobot/multilingual-tutoring-and-accessibility) |
| **Outcome impact** | **Timely support:** lowers frustration and drop-off, **results:** higher mastery and completion. | Reporting KPIs, proving value to stakeholders. | [How Rigobot improves completion rates](/rigobot/how-rigobot-improves-completion-rates) |

## Publish & export

Publish your course where your learners already are. LearnPack will let you share a public or private link, export SCORM for any LMS, embed via iFrame, or generate an ePub for offline reading. All exports preserve structure, assessments, and assets, so you keep the same learning experience with proper versioning and access control.
